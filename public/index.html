Fractal explorer

Pan the fractal either by dragging or with the arrow keys. Zoom with two pointers or with space and backspace.

You can "sharpen" or "roughen" the fractal with the slider that controls pixel iterations. You can also change the multibrot exponent continuously by rotating the screw or as integers with the attached buttons.

Once you zoom deep enough and the color scale changes, you are outside the precision of native floating point numbers. You may still try it with the multibrot exponent 2.0 and, depending on the device, things may work due to the efforts of algorithmically extending the precision.
